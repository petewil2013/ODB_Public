About Us

Drawing from the timeless words “Give us this day our daily bread,” we create handcrafted sourdough that honors tradition, craftsmanship, and community.

Our Daily Bread is a Catholic-inspired artisan bakery that unites craftsmanship, faith, and community in every loaf. Our storefront will offer pickup orders, dine-in options, and communal events—from baking workshops to live music and educational talks.

We partner with Simply Bread to bring you the finest naturally leavened bread, baked with care and delivered fresh.
